Undergraduate Thesis

"Subsoil Parameter Analysis Through Geo-BIM Integrated Soil Profiling in Khulna City"

February 2020-April 2022

  • The goal of the study was to develop a vertical soil pofile using Geo-BIM tools. Total 16 number of soil test report with a large number of boreholes data from different places of Khulna city were collected for this research and software compliant borehole data was prepared in excel. Location details of boreholes was collected from google map and ground surface level was considered as plain surface level. After importing data into the Geo-BIM tools, the subsurface model was developed. By interpolating field data virtual Boreholes were also prepared for filling up the large gaps between boreholes.
  • After that, Validation was done comparing with field data to the software data by omitting some boreholes from some different places and comparison graph was prepared
